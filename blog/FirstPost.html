First Post

This post marks the beginning of my personal blogging journey.

Technology, Music and Books

These are the three things I am passionate about in life. Technology being my professional direction. Music because I am a trained tabla player. (TaalGyan). Although I've been reading since childhood, it is a new found passion.
I intend to make this blog an outlet for my ideas and thoughts. I hope this public journal forms an accurate and authentic window for people to peep into my life as I go about experiencing it. I do not know what this blog might evolve into, or if it would even stay alive for long. All I know is that I wanted to write a blog and so here I am. Welcome to my world.
