First Post


This post marks the beginning of my personal blogging journey.

Technology, Music and Books

These are possibly the only three things I am passionate about in life. Technology being my professional direction. Music because I am a trained tabla player. (TaalGyan). Now that I think of it, calling books a passion might be too early. Although I have been reading since childhood, it was only very recently that I started finding reading an activity that I thoroughly enjoy.

I intend to make this blog an outlet for my ideas and thoughts and hope that this public journal forms an accurate and authentic window for people to peep into my life as I go about experiencing it. I do not know what this blog might evolve into, or if it would even stay alive for long. All I know is that I wanted to write a blog and so here I am. Welcome to my world.
